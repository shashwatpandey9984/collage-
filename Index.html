About Us

Established in 2016, Government Polytechnic Chopan has rapidly grown as a leading institution in technical education under the Directorate of Technical Education, Uttar Pradesh. With modern labs, expert faculty, and industry-driven curriculum, the institute empowers students for future careers in Computer Science, Electronics, and Automobile Engineering.
